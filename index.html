Bulls And Cows

Guess The Secret Number





Rules:

To win you must guess the secret number.
Valid guesses are exactly 4 digits.
Invalid guesses include: Duplicating numbers (ex. 6772), not a 4 digit guess,
and anything that's not a number.
If you get one bull it means you have one of the four digits in the correct spot.
If you get one cow it means you have one of the four digits correct but, not in the correct spot.
And so on if you get more bulls and cows.

Good Luck!
